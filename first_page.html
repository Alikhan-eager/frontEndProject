Qosh keldinizder bul saitqa

Bul sait arkyly siz qazaq tilin uirenip, emin-erkin soilei alasyz.
Bul course kimdeerge arnalgan :
eger siz shet el azamaty bolsanyz,
qazaq tiinde soileuden qatelikter zhiberetin bolsanyz
onda bizge kosylynyz

A-0 dengei

Bul a-0 dengei, siz bul dengeide alippeden bastap uirenesiz.Aripterdin dybystaluy, zhazulyyn uirenetin bolasyz

A-1 dengei

Bul a-1 dengei, siz bul dengeide soz taptaryn uirenesiz,sozderdin kurylymyn karastyratyn bolasyz.

B-1 dengei

Bul b-1 dengei, sizder bul dengeide soz kurylymy, kuramy, sozder arasyndagy bailanysty otetin bolasyz.

B-1+ dengei

Bul b-1+ dengei, siz bul dengeide, kurdeli soilemder, kurau, adamdarmen karym katynastagy sozder kuaraly.

C-1 dengei

Bul c-1 dengei, siz bul dengeide, til okilderimen bailanys zhasau, olarmen keri bailanys zhane ui zhymystary.

C-1+ dengei

Bul c-1+ dengei, siz bul dengeide qazaq tilinde emin erkin soilep, ozinizdi qazaqpyn dep esepteisizder.
